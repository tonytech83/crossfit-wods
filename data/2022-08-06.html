Circle Training (Interval Training)

40 seconds ON / 15 seconds OFF

3 rounds of:

10 burpee box jump over 

15 wall ball (9/6 kg.)

15 abs roller row

Rest 3 minutes then

3 rounds of:

40 battle rope 

10 shuttle run (10 meters)

16 overhead walking lunges with plate (15/10 kg.)

Rest 3 minutes then

3 rounds of:

5 wall walk 

15 toes to bar 

10/8 assault bike calories

Rest 3 minutes between sets
